LP-DevTool comes in three plans: **Basis**, **Creator** and **Pro**. Each plan unlocks more of the builder toolkit, from your first client-side scripts all the way to the full fullstack arsenal with NUI tools.

<Note>
  As long as the beta is running, **every account has all features of all plans unlocked**. You do not need an upgrade and you do not pay anything. Booking and payment will follow later.
</Note>

## What each plan offers

Every higher plan includes everything from the one below it and adds more tools on top.

### Basis (free)

The entry point for getting to know the builder and creating your first resources.

- **Lua Builder (client)**: build client-side FiveM scripts via drag and build
- **Loading Screen Builder**: design custom loading screens
- **HUD preview**: view HUD layouts live
- **Community Store**: browse templates shared by the community
- **250 MB cloud storage** for your projects

### Creator (€9 / month)

For everyone who wants to build not just on the client but also on the server side.

- **Everything in Basis**
- **Lua Builder with server blocks**: build client and server logic
- **Export your own resources**: download finished FiveM resources
- **Loading Screen Builder with mouse effects**
- **Up to 25 saved scripts**
- **1 GB cloud storage**

### Pro (€19 / month)

The complete toolkit for demanding projects and your own interfaces.

- **Everything in Creator**
- **Lua Builder as fullstack**: client, server and fullstack logic in one
- **Easy NUI Builder**: build menus, overlays and interactive HUDs via drag and drop
- **Notification Builder**: design your own in-game notifications
- **Loading Screen Builder with 3D models**
- **Unlimited scripts**
- **5 GB cloud storage**

## Everything is unlocked during the beta

So you can fully test the platform and give feedback, the plan boundaries do not apply during the beta.

- Every builder is usable without an upgrade, from Basis to Pro.
- Exporting as a finished FiveM resource is active for all accounts.
- You can try every feature and framework without restrictions.

<Note>
  Inside the app you will find an overview of the future plans under **Upgrade**. During the beta each plan shows "Included in the beta", so no upgrade is needed.
</Note>

## Becoming a beta tester

Spots are limited so the team can process high-quality feedback. Here is how to join:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Discord">
    Join the [LP-Scripts Discord](https://discord.com/invite/W7uzcDCKnJ). Everything around the beta happens there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the right role">
    Think about which plan matches your level of knowledge. Each one moves the platform forward in its own way:

    - **Basis**: for anyone without coding experience who simply wants to build scripts. Your task: use the tool and check whether you understand everything without help.
    - **Creator**: for users who have seen scripts before and can fix small mistakes. Your task: check whether the generated Lua scripts work in-game and the block logic is correct.
    - **Pro**: for experienced developers with knowledge of Lua architecture, HTML or modern web design. Your task: analyze code structure, performance and developer experience in depth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply through the ticket system">
    Applications run through the ticket system on the Discord. Briefly describe which role suits you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the beta terms and get started">
    Read the beta terms, then the full builder is open to you, with all features from Basis, Creator and Pro.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Because the platform is in beta, it is meant for trying things out, building and giving feedback, and is not intended for production use on a live server.
</Note>
