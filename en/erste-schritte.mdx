LP-DevTool is a web-based toolkit for FiveM scripts: you assemble your resource visually,
save it, and export it as a finished resource. All you need to get started is a Discord
account: this page takes you from your first login to your first builder.

## Sign in with Discord

Signing in works entirely through Discord. There is no separate password to remember.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Open LP-DevTool. You land on the welcome screen with the message
    “Willkommen bei LP-DevTool" (Welcome to LP-DevTool).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Discord">
    Click **Mit Discord anmelden** (Sign in with Discord). You are redirected to Discord,
    where you confirm access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Back to LP-DevTool">
    After confirming, you are sent back automatically and land directly in your dashboard
    (the **Übersicht** / Overview page).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your account is how you sign in, save your scripts, and export them as a finished
  resource. Your display name and avatar are taken from Discord.
</Note>

## Your dashboard (Overview)

Right after logging in you see the **Overview**: your personal starting point with a
greeting and the subtitle “Your overview of scripts, activity and account".

### The four key figures

At the top, four cards summarize your account:

- **My Scripts**: how many scripts you have already created.
- **Activities**: how often you have been active in LP-DevTool (e.g. scripts you saved
  or exported).
- **Membership**: your current tier: **Basis**, **Creator** or **Pro**.
- **Last activity**: the date of your most recent action.

### Other sections on the Overview

- **Activity chart**: shows your activity over time. You can switch between the
  **24h**, **7d** and **30d** ranges.
- **News**: the latest updates about LP-DevTool.
- **Latest activity**: a chronological list of your most recent actions.

<Note>
  A freshly created account starts with no scripts of its own. As soon as you save your
  first build, the key figures and the activity list fill up automatically.
</Note>

## Getting to the builders

You'll find the navigation in the **sidebar** on the left. It's split into groups: the
builders live in the **Builder** group.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sidebar">
    Look at the left-hand sidebar. You can collapse and expand it to make more room for
    your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the “Builder" group">
    Under the **Builder** group you'll find all available toolkits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a builder">
    Click the builder you want to start working with right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Workspace & other sections

Besides the builders, the sidebar also gives you access to:

- **Overview**: the dashboard you just landed on.
- **My Scripts**: all your saved scripts in one place.
- **Store**: browse templates and building blocks.
- **Activity**: the full history of your actions.
- **Documentation**: this help, straight from the app.

## Account menu

At the very bottom of the sidebar you'll find your profile with avatar, display name and
your membership tier. A single click opens a menu with the following entries:

- **Member since**: the date you registered.
- **My Scripts**: quick access to your saved scripts.
- **Help and Docs**: jump into the documentation.
- **Sign out**: signs you out of your account after a confirmation prompt.

<Note>
  After signing out, you'll need to sign in with Discord again to regain access to your
  scripts.
</Note>
